Design Processes

Types of Visualization

  • Heatmap: Billboard Top 100 Ranked Holiday songs by decade (tooltip to show title, artist and rank)
  • Bar chart: most frequent words in the song clicked on from the heatmap
  • Word cloud: similar to the bar chart, shows most frequent words in the song previously clicked on while visualizing the frequency by size instead of length
  • Node-link diagram: shows songs in which the chosen word exist (from the bar chart or the word cloud)

Decision Making Process (on Types of Vis to Use)

  • Heatmap was our top choice for visualizing the top song since it is great for displaying 3 variables at the same time (song title, year, ranking).
  • Heatmap is also ideal for on-click interactions since the squares offer larger area for user to click on than, for example, a line chart.
  • Bar chart was almost a given when we started the project since bar charts are clear and efficient in visualizing a 2-variable subset.
  • Word cloud was a natural choice when we decided to build a vis with lyrics.
  • Node-link diagram was inspired by the idea of decorating a Christmas tree with the nodes as ornaments.

Struggles

  • We were blindsided by the lack of lyrics data since in the beginning it looked sufficient as the dataset was large.
  • Being in different timezones made communication less efficient.
  • Initially we had a hard time enabling the nodes to move around while being confined in a designate
